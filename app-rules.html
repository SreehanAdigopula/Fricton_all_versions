Friction Rulebook

App Rules

The simple rules Friction uses to track focus, adapt your timer, and grow your buddy.

Quick Idea

The app rewards cleaner focus and gently adjusts when sessions get messy.

Focus Session Rules

  • Start Session: A focus block begins when you press Start Session.
  • Complete Session: Mark a session completed when you stayed with the work until the timer ends or the goal is done.
  • Failed Session: Mark failed when the session no longer counts as real focus time.
  • Timer Adaptation: Friction can shorten or extend the next session based on your recent focus quality.

Distractions & Breaks

  • Distraction Limit: A session allows up to 3 distractions before it becomes too distracted.
  • Break Limit: A session allows up to 3 breaks before the app treats the next session more gently.
  • Honest Tracking: The app works best when distractions, breaks, completions, and failures are marked honestly.

Rewards & Penalties

  • Clean Streak Reward: 3 clean sessions in a row can unlock bonus focus time.
  • Distraction Penalty: Too many distractions can reset the next session to a shorter focus block.
  • Break Penalty: Too many breaks can shorten the next focus session by a small amount.
  • Fail Streak Penalty: Repeated failed sessions can reduce the next session length so restarting feels easier.

Pet Progress Rules

  • Growth Milestones: Pets grow when you complete enough weekly sessions with low distractions.
  • Three Stages: Each buddy has Baby, Teen, and Adult levels.
  • Appearance Choice: You can choose your buddy animal in Settings, but progress still depends on focus behavior.
  • Weekly Rhythm: Weekly stats guide growth and can be reset when you want a fresh start.
